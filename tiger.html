ANIMALS MAP PLACES

TIGER

Tigers are majestic predators, known for their striking orange and black stripes. They're apex hunters, feeding on a variety of prey in their habitats. With several subspecies, tigers range from the Sundarbans' mangrove forests to Russia's birch forests.

Unfortunately, tigers face significant threats, including habitat loss, poaching, and human-wildlife conflict, leading to endangered status for many subspecies. Conservation efforts focus on protecting habitats, reducing human-tiger conflict, and combating wildlife trafficking.
